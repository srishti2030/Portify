Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Portify?

To get started with Portify, simply sign up for an account on our website. Once registered, you can select the porting service you need, provide the necessary details (e.g., account numbers, provider information), and initiate the process. Our easy-to-follow guides will help you through each step.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! Portify offers a free trial for new users. This allows you to test our platform’s features and see how it fits your needs. During the trial period, you can use a limited number of ports or transfers to assess the platform’s capabilities.

How long does the porting process take?

The time it takes to complete the porting process depends on the type of service and the complexity of the transfer. Generally, number porting can take anywhere from a few hours to 5 business days. Data or subscription migrations may take longer depending on the volume and service provider’s response time.

What happens if the porting process is delayed?

While most porting requests are completed within the estimated time frame, delays can occur due to factors like the response time of the provider, issues with account details, or technical difficulties. If there is a delay, Portify's customer support team will contact you and work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Can I use Portify for international porting?

Yes, Portify supports international porting for certain services and countries. Check our list of supported regions or contact customer support for more information about international porting availability and specific requirements.
